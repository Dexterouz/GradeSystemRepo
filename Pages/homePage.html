Also term "conflict prevention" is an intervention that is designed to prevent the start or resumption of violent conflict by creating a sustainable peace. It addresses the root cause of the conflict, create a societal expectation for peaceful conflict resolution and ensure that the civilians have freedom from fear(negative peace), freedom from want(positive peace) and freedom from humanitarian during and after conflict.

The task included in peace building vary depending on the situation and the agent of peace building. Successful peace building create rule of law mechanism, prevent conflict from restarting.

Peace keeping refers to activities that tend to create conditions that favours lasting peace.The general understanding about peace keepers is that they monitor and observe peace process in post conflict areas and may assist ex-combatant in implementing peace agreement commitment that the have undertaken and such assistance may come in many forms such as confidence building measures, power-sharing arrangement, electoral support, strengthening the rule of law and economic and social development

The united nation (UN peace keepers) they are refer to as the “blue berets” or “blue helmets” , and the personnel involve are soldier, police officers and civilian personnel. Non-UN peace keepers include the NATO mission in Kosovo.
